3. Aston Martin Vanquish - Offers remarkable agility, grip, and speed with a dramatic driver-focused cabin and a spectacular-sounding twin-turbo V8

8. Porsche 911 Carrera S - Features 473 horsepower, 390 pound-feet of torque, and a blistering 3.1-second zero-to-60 time

9. Ferrari 12Cilindri - One of the last big V-12-powered grand tourers, representing a nearly extinct breed of performance car

10. Hyundai IONIQ 5 N - The first EV to truly deliver a performance car experience for all the senses, making lap times just as rewarding as any ICE car
